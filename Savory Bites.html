Our Story

Founded in 2018, Savory Bites brings together a passion for local ingredients and international culinary techniques. Our head chef, Marco Bianchi, combines his Italian heritage with modern gastronomy to create unforgettable dining experiences.

We source 95% of our ingredients from local farms and suppliers within a 50-mile radius, ensuring the freshest flavors while supporting our community.
